History of Flat Design

Flat design has evolved from earlier graphic principles into the popular digital aesthetic we see today. Here is a brief timeline:

1940s

Swiss Style dominates the scene with grids, sans-serif typography, and clean hierarchy. Heavily influenced by minimalism.

2006

Microsoft releases the Zune — lowercase typography + strong color fields.

2010

Windows Phone 7 introduces Metro design — bright grids, sans-serif, flat icons.

2012

Windows 8 launches with Metro's full aesthetic — blocks, sharp edges, bold colors.

2013

Apple releases iOS 7. Skeuomorphism disappears almost overnight across the web.
