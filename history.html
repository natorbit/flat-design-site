History of Flat Design

1940s — Swiss Style

Dominates visual design with grids, sans-serif typography, and minimalism.

2006 — Microsoft Zune

Large lowercase typography and bold backgrounds influence future UI trends.

2010 — Windows Phone 7

Bright tiles, sans-serif type, flat icons. Introduces “Metro” design.

2012 — Windows 8

Metro showcased in full: sharp edges, block grids, bright color.

2013 — Apple iOS 7

Apple moves away from skeuomorphism. Flat design becomes industry standard.
