History of Flat Design

Flat design has evolved from earlier design principles into the popular aesthetic we see today. Here is a brief timeline:

1940s — Swiss Style

Dominates the scene with grids, sans-serif typography, and clean hierarchy. Heavily influenced by minimalism.

2006 — Microsoft Zune

Releases Zune media player — large, lowercase typography and background imagery.

2010 — Windows Phone 7

Large, bright grids, sans-serif, flat icons. Dubbed "Metro" design.

2012 — Windows 8

Metro design in full: block grid layout, sharp edges, bright colours and large background images.

2013 — Apple iOS 7

Apple releases iOS 7 — flat design helps replace skeuomorphic approaches; skeuomorphism quickly becomes outdated.
